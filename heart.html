Signs of a Heart Attack

Recognizing symptoms early can save lives

Home

Common Symptoms

  • Chest pain or discomfort, often described as pressure, squeezing, or fullness
  • Shortness of breath, with or without chest pain
  • Pain or discomfort in the arms, neck, jaw, back, or stomach
  • Cold sweat, nausea, or dizziness

When to Seek Emergency Care

If you or someone else experiences these symptoms, call emergency services (911) immediately. Every second counts!

How to Reduce Your Risk

  • Maintain a healthy diet and regular exercise
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption
  • Manage stress and get regular medical checkups
